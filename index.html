Gamer Gazer

React, Redux, Flask, PostgreSQL, SQLAlchemy

Gamer Gazer is a full-stack web app that gives users the ability to review video games and add games to their library. The general audience for this app are users with video games on multiple platforms or users with too many games to count. Whether you're looking for a new game to play or trying to remember if you ever finished that game you downloaded two years ago, Gamer Gazer can get you started.

Shamstagram

React, Redux, Flask, PostgreSQL

Shamstagram allows users to share images, like and comment on other’s images, and follow other users. A logged in user will see a list of posts from users they follow, and once they have scrolled long enough, Shamstagram starts to introduce some new posts for the user to interact with.

Momentum

Express, Pug, Sequelize

Momentum is a website where users can share travel destinations, recommendations, and interact with other travelers’ posts. It allows users to plan trips based on other users' experiences. Users can find posts of their intended location and find a list of comments loaded with information about where to eat or visit.
